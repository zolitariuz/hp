HP Indigo 5600

A versatile press with leading digital offset quality for the growing commercial business.

The HP Indigo 5600 Digital Press delivers unmatched digital offset and photo quality with exceptional versatility and high productivity. Seamlessly switching between jobs and media types—with a new added ability to print on synthetics—the press is ideal for printers with a diverse job mix.
